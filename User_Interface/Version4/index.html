How to Use the Sliders

To help us find the best room for you, adjust the sliders below based on your preferences:

  • Main Slider: Use this slider to set your desired level for each parameter (e.g., temperature, air quality, noise). The description above the slider will update dynamically to reflect your choice.
  • Importance Slider: Adjust this slider to indicate how critical the selected parameter is to you. Higher importance means this parameter will have more weight in the room selection process.
  • Flexibility Slider: Use this slider to specify how much variation from your selected value you're willing to accept. For example, if you prefer a specific temperature, you can allow slight or significant deviations based on your flexibility.

Tips: Choose higher importance for factors that significantly affect your comfort or productivity. Set flexibility to low if you require precise conditions, or increase flexibility to allow more room options.
